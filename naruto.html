About Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

Main Characters

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is a ninja from the village of Konohagakure. He is energetic, enthusiastic, and always willing to help his friends. His dream is to become Hokage, the strongest ninja in the village.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is one of Naruto's closest friends and rivals. He is a talented ninja from the Uchiha clan, seeking revenge against his brother who killed his entire family.

Sakura Haruno

Sakura Haruno is one of Naruto's teammates. She is highly intelligent and has incredible chakra control, allowing her to become a powerful medical ninja under the training of Tsunade.
