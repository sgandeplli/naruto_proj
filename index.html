Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha is a fictional character from the popular anime and manga series Naruto, created by Masashi Kishimoto. He is known for his intelligence, combat skills, and complex personality.

As a member of the Uchiha clan and later the Akatsuki, Itachi is renowned for his Sharingan and powerful techniques, including the use of genjutsu.

Despite being perceived as a villain, Itachi's true motivations are revealed to be more nuanced, showcasing his sacrifice for the sake of his brother, Sasuke Uchiha.

Key Facts:

  • Full Name: Itachi Uchiha
  • Birthday: June 9
  • Affiliation: Konohagakure, Akatsuki
  • Notable Techniques: Sharingan, Amaterasu, Tsukuyomi
